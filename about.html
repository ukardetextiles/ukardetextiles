who we are

Ukarde Textiles Group


Founded by Mr. Ankush Y Ukarde and Mr. Vivekanand Y Ukarde, Ukarde Textiles Group has been successful in bringing utmost perfection in our products from our establishment in 2019. We focus on 100% customer satisfaction since we have the experience to service international customers. Grey cotton fabric is widely used in sheeting, as well as in a variety of industrial applications such as Banners, Construction, Filtration, Flags, Military, Packaging, Safety & Protective, Tents, Abrasives, Coatings, Composites, and so on. All of these applications make use of our grey cotton fabric
